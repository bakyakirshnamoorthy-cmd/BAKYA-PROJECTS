About the System

The Emergency Blood Fetcher Management System is a life-saving platform that connects voluntary blood donors with hospitals during medical emergencies.

Our system helps hospitals quickly find compatible blood donors in their area when they need it most, reducing critical response time and potentially saving lives.

By registering as a donor, you join a network of lifesavers ready to help when emergencies arise. Hospitals can efficiently manage blood requests and maintain donation records.

Key Features

  • Donor registration with OTP verification
  • Hospital registration and secure login
  • Search donors by location and blood group
  • Send emergency blood requests via email
  • Donation confirmation with OTP system
  • Automatic 90-day donor mute after donation
  • Complete blood donation history records
